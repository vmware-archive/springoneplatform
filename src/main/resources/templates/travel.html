Hotel Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are allowed up to seventy-two (72) hours prior to arrival without penalty. Cancellations within seventy-two (72) hours of the scheduled arrival date or failure to check in will be charged one night’s room and tax to the credit card provided at the time of booking.

Early Departures

The Aria allows guests to revise their departure date at check-in with no fee; however, once checked in, early departures will be charged in full for all nights of the reservation.

Check-In/Check-Out Times

Check-in time starts at 3:00 pm. Check-out time is 11:00 am.
